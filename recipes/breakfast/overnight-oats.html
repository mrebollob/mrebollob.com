Overnight Oats

Ingredientes

Instrucciones

  1. In a 16oz wide-mouth mason jar (or similar container), combine all ingredients.
  2. Stir thoroughly until fully combined.
  3. Wipe the rim clean, seal, and refrigerate overnight.
  4. Next morning, stir again. If too thick, add a teaspoon or two of milk and mix.

Notas

  • Use rolled oats, not steel-cut — steel-cut need multiple nights to soften.
  • Flavored yogurt works; vanilla can be overpowering.
  • Lower-fat yogurt gives a thinner result; reduce milk slightly to compensate.
  • Honey, brown sugar, or sugar-free alternatives all work as sweetener.
  • Keeps until end of week if sealed; quality declines after 5 days as oats get mushy.
