Overnight Oats

Base Recipe

Ingredientes

Instrucciones

  1. In a 16oz wide-mouth mason jar (or similar container), combine all ingredients.
  2. Stir thoroughly until fully combined.
  3. Wipe the rim clean, seal, and refrigerate overnight.
  4. Next morning, stir again. If too thick, add a teaspoon or two of milk and mix.

Notas

  • Use rolled oats, not steel-cut — steel-cut need multiple nights to soften.
  • Flavored yogurt works; vanilla can be overpowering.
  • Lower-fat yogurt gives a thinner result; reduce milk slightly to compensate.
  • Keeps until end of week if sealed; quality declines after 5 days as oats get mushy.

Variations

Peanut Butter Cup

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Seal and refrigerate overnight.

Mocha Latte (minus the Mocha)

  1. Mix the oats, milk, ¼ cup yogurt, chia seeds, maple syrup, cocoa powder, and ½ tsp vanilla together.
  2. Separately, mix the 2 tbsp yogurt with ¼ tsp vanilla — this is the topping.
  3. Seal the jar and refrigerate overnight.
  4. Next morning, add the vanilla yogurt on top before serving.

Banana Bread

  1. Mix oats, milk, yogurt, chia seeds, honey, mashed banana half, cinnamon, and nutmeg together.
  2. Seal and refrigerate overnight.
  3. Next morning, top with the chopped banana half, crushed nuts, and mini chocolate chips if using.

Peanut Butter and Jelly

  1. Mix all ingredients except the toppings together.
  2. Seal and refrigerate overnight.
  3. Next morning, top with crushed peanuts and chopped strawberries.

Dark Chocolate Delight

  1. Mix all ingredients except the toppings together.
  2. Seal and refrigerate overnight.
  3. Next morning, top with crushed raspberries and shaved chocolate.
