About Wada Sanzo

Wada Sanzo (1883-1967) was a Japanese painter, costume designer and researcher who devoted his life to the study of color theory. He believed that color was not just a decorative element, but a powerful tool for communication and expression.

This app brings his timeless color combinations to your mobile device, making it easy to explore and apply these harmonious palettes in your creative projects.

Based on "A Dictionary of Color Combinations Vol. 1" - a collection of 348 color combinations based on 159 colors from the original 6-volume work published from 1933 to 1934.
