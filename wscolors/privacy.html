Privacy Policy

W.S. Colors App

Last updated: October 1, 2025

Overview

W.S. Colors ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Don't Collect

W.S. Colors is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device information beyond what's necessary for app functionality
  • Location data
  • Photos or camera data (camera access is used only for color scanning and is processed locally)
  • Usage patterns or behavioral data
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

Third-Party Services

While we don't collect personal data ourselves, our app uses the following third-party services for essential functionality:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand general app usage patterns and improve our service. Google Analytics may collect:

  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • App performance metrics
  • General demographic information

You can learn more about Google Analytics' privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy

Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to monitor app stability and fix crashes. Crashlytics may collect:

  • Crash reports and technical diagnostics
  • Device and app version information
  • Anonymous identifiers for crash tracking

You can learn more about Firebase's privacy practices at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Camera Access

The app requests camera access solely for the color scanning feature. All camera processing is done locally on your device, and no images or camera data are transmitted, stored, or shared.

Favorite Colors

When you mark colors as favorites, this information is stored locally on your device and optionally synced through your device's cloud service (iCloud for iOS). We do not have access to this data.

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there's minimal risk to your privacy. Any data processed by third-party services is handled according to their respective privacy policies and security measures.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
